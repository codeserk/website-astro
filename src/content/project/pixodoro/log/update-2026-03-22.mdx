Time to make some noise! A timer without sounds is just... a clock.

Added customizable sounds for phase transitions - so you know when it's time to take a break (or get back to work). There's also a ticking sound during active sessions, which is surprisingly motivating. Volume control is there too, in case you don't want to annoy your coworkers.

I also added keyboard shortcuts: `space` to toggle the timer, plus shortcuts to skip and reset. These felt essential - if you're in the zone, you don't want to reach for the mouse.
